# Example — vertical-clip

## Input

> I have a 3-minute talking-head video of me explaining why I left my corporate job to build on Base. Key moments: 0:12 — "I was making good money but I felt like I was building someone else's dream." 0:45 — "The moment I deployed my first smart contract, something clicked." 1:30 — "I'm not going to lie, the first 6 months were terrifying." 2:10 — "Now I wake up and I'm excited to open my laptop." Filmed vertically on iPhone, decent lighting, no background music.

## Output

**1. Hook Analysis**
Strongest moment: *"I was making good money but I felt like I was building someone else's dream."* (0:12)
This is the hook — it creates immediate tension and relatability. Start here, cold open, no intro. Cut everything before it.
Suggested hook duration: 0–3s, slam cut directly into this line mid-sentence if possible.

**2. Video Structure**
- **0–3s:** Cold open — "I was making good money but I felt like I was building someone else's dream."
- **3–8s:** Jump cut to — "The moment I deployed my first smart contract, something clicked." (payoff to the tension)
- **8–18s:** "I'm not going to lie, the first 6 months were terrifying." (vulnerability = retention spike)
- **18–28s:** "Now I wake up and I'm excited to open my laptop." (resolution)
- **28–35s:** CTA overlay

**3. Caption & On-screen Text**
- Hook text on screen at 0s: **"I was making good money..."** — large, bold, centered, white with black stroke
- At 0:45 moment: **"something clicked 🔥"** — smaller, bottom third
- At 1:30: **"6 months of terror"** — red or orange text, punchy
- Animation: word-by-word pop-in, not full sentence at once. Match speech rhythm.

**4. Editing Direction**
- Jump cuts every 2–4s — remove all pauses and filler words
- Slight zoom push (1.0 → 1.05) on the emotional peaks
- Speed ramp: very slight slow-mo (0.9x) on "something clicked"
- No transitions — straight cuts only for talking head
- Crop to 9:16, keep face in upper 60% of frame

**5. Music & Audio Suggestion**
Low, subtle background track — cinematic lo-fi or ambient. Keep it at -20dB, this story carries itself. Avoid anything with lyrics. A single soft piano riff would work well under the "terrifying" section.

**6. Final CTA / Ending**
End on the "excited to open my laptop" line, then:
> Text overlay: "If this resonates — follow for more."
No talking after the last beat. Let the music fade.

**7. Expected Performance**
High retention potential. The cold open hook has strong scroll-stop because it opens with earned money + unfulfillment — universally relatable, not just crypto. The vulnerability at 1:30 will cause a retention spike. Estimated average watch time: 70–80% if the first 3s land. Rewatch likely from people who want to share it.
